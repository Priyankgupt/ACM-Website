About Us



GEU

Graphic Era Deemed to be University(GEU), formerly known as Graphic Era Institute of Technology, is one of the leading universities in developing India as it has become a popular university in a concise span of time. It has also been ranked 64th by the NIRF in 2021 rankings. The university offers Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Doctoral programs in streams like Engineering, Management, Computer Application, Humanities & Social Sciences, Commerce, Life Sciences, Hotel management, and Hospitality. Among these the most popular courses are B.Tech, B.Sc, and B.E.




ACM

ACM stands for Association for Computing Machinery, aims to bring together the computing educators, researchers, and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges.ACM strengthens the profession's collective voice through strong leadership, promotion of the highest standards, and recognition of technical excellence. ACM supports the professional growth of its members by providing opportunities for life‚Äêlong learning, career development, and professional networking. ACM provides a platform to the students and the pofessionals as well to showcase their leadership skills via Student and Professional Chapters, that create networking possibilities and enhance career development. It offers volunteer roles for its members and non-members as well.




GEU ACM

GEU holds ACM's Student Chapter as GEU ACM Student Chapter which is a student-run organization that aims to promote the wholesome development of students in the various spheres of lives. We aim to provide a stage for students to learn and grow as an individual and as a team as well in their field of interests. The chapter firmly believes that quality is better than quantity, and based on that we have some specific domains on which we work together for the development of the team and the society as well. The events we conduct are purely with the mindset that each and every participant must have something fruitful at the end of it.


GEU ACM-W

The ACM community is as diverse as the subfields that comprise computer science, from educators and researchers in academia to practitioners in project management, industrial research, and software development, engineering, and application design. This diversity extends to their gender and ethnicities with its Women's Chapters.
GEU ACM-W Student Chapter, strongly believes that there is no force equal to a woman determined to rise. We have witnessed ourselves as a leader in different sectors and now its time to represent ourselves in our own college and in the communities in outside world as strong individuals. GEU ACM-W celebrates and tends to amplify women empowerment, while inspiring, and uplifting all the women out there for the world to hear us roar louder, watch us soar higher!!
